Mu Mateka: Tariki ya 3 Werurwe

Tariki 3 Werurwe ni umunsi wa 63 mu minsi igize umwaka usigaje indi 302 ngo ugere ku musozo.

Bimwe mu byabaye kuri iyi tariki mu mateka

1845: Florida yemewe nka leta ya 27 mu zigize Leta Zunze Ubumwe z’Amerika.

1857: Mu ntambara ya kabiri, u Bufaransa n’u Bwongereza byatangije intambara mu Bushinwa.

1915: Ikigo NACA cyaje guhinduka NASA cyarashinzwe.

1923: Ikinyamakuru The Times cyo mu Bwongereza cyasohotse ku nshuro ya mbere.

1924: Leta yigenga ya Fiume yiyunze ku gice cy’ubwami bw’u Butaliyani.

1931: Nibwo Leta Zunze Ubumwe z’Amerika zemeje ’The Star-Spangled Banner’ nk’indirimbo yubahiriza igihugu.

1938; Havumbuwe peteroli muri Arabie Saoudite.

1942 : Mu ntambara ya Kabiri y’Isi, indege icumi zo mu Buyapani zarashe mu mujyi wa Broome muri Australia zica abantu barenze 100.

1958 : Nibwo Nuri as-Said yatorewe kuba minisitiri w’intebe wa Iraq ku nshuro ya 14.

2002 : Abaturage bake bo mu Busuwisi batoye ko igihugu cyabo kijya mu Muryango w’Abibumbye.

Bamwe mu bavutse kuri iyi tariki

1455: John II, Umwami wa Portugal.

1778: Frederica w’i Mecklenburg, Umwamikazi w’i Hanover.

1982: Derreck Robinson, umukinnyi w’umupira w’amaguru w’Umunyamerika.

Bamwe mu batabarutse kuri iyi tariki

1606: Nyaungyan Min, Umwami wa Burma.

1717: Pierre Allix, umupasitori w’Umufaransa.

2008: Norman Smith, umuririmbyi akaba n’umuproducer w’Umwongereza .
